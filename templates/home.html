Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SoftwareAI

How does SoftwareAI differ from general AI assistants?

Unlike general AI assistants, each SoftwareAI agent is specifically trained for a particular coding task or domain. This specialization allows for more accurate, contextual, and helpful assistance tailored to each specific area of software development.

Is my code secure when using SoftwareAI?

Absolutely. Your code never leaves our secure environment. We don't store your code beyond the active session unless you explicitly enable history features in the premium plan. We follow industry best practices for data security and encryption.

Can I switch between different AI agents?

Yes, you can easily switch between agents during a conversation or start new conversations with specific agents. Premium users have access to all agents, while free users can access three of their choosing.

What programming languages are supported?

SoftwareAI supports all major programming languages including JavaScript, TypeScript, Python, Java, C#, Ruby, Go, PHP, Swift, Kotlin, and many more. Our agents are continuously trained on the latest language features and frameworks.

How do I upgrade from free to premium?

Simply click on the "Upgrade Now" button on your dashboard or the pricing page. You'll be guided through a simple checkout process, and your account will be instantly upgraded with all premium features.
